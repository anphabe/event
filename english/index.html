Ms. THANH NGUYỄN

CEO & Chief Happiness Officer
Anphabe

Ms. Thanh Nguyen is the CEO & Chief Happiness Officer of Anphabe – the pioneer in Vietnam in providing integrated Employer Branding (EB) & Happy Workforce solutions. Pursuing the mission of “Building Vietnam Happy Workforce”, in August 2017, Thanh Nguyen has had the honor to be the first Vietnamese to receive Asia HRD Awards for “Contribution to HR Community”, organized by HRD Asia Congress.

Prior to her commitment at Anphabe, Thanh has had unique experiences as Regional Marketing Manager at Unilever Asia, Africa, and Middle East region at Unilever and later, as the Founder & CEO of an HR service subsidiary under Navigos Group.

Global Performance Coach
ALAIN GOUDSMET

Mentally Fit Global

Global Performance Coach Alain Goudsmet is one of the most influential coaches in sports, business & human performance coaching. He is am international renowned expert in mental coaching, performance coaching & leadership coaching. Coach Alain used to be the Mental Coach of Belgian Olympic athletes and teams.

Coach Alain is the Top Executive Coach for Global Leaders, C-suite teams and International Boards ofMultinational Corporations in the list of Fortune 500 & Top Global Brands, such as Amazon, Alibaba, AirFrance-KLM, Microsoft, Pfizer, Bayer, Unilever, Coca-Cola, Heineken, ING Bank, Norvo Nordisk, Nike, Adidas, Tata, … He is also the Senior Faculty of INSEAD (Singapore and France) in MBA and Executive Education programs& also in other prestigious business schools such as ICHEC, Solvay Business School... Apart from that, he is a TEDx Speaker & author of many books and researches on Leadership, Corporate Culture Transformation, Energy Management, Business Efficiency Improvement, High Performing Team, ...

Human Performance Coach
IRAIS BROWAEYS

Mentally Fit Global

Human Performance Coach Irais Browaeys has always been passionate about sports and psychology. She used to be the Marketing Manager in the business of sports and leisure, travelling all over Europe, working with different pro cycling and triathlon teams, responsible for the biggest sports events in Europe (Tour of Britain, Challenge Rimini IT, Tour de Pologne, Challenge Salou ES, Göteborg Marathon,…).

As a Certified Integrative Psychotherapist, a Certified Burn-out Coach and a passionate triathlete, she embodies the spirit of endurance sports and the mental game that goes with it. Combining her integrative perspective to develop people and her personal experience in sports and business, she authentically motivates peoples and teams to become energized, build a winning culture and to perform at their highest potential.

Associate Director
Intage Vietnam LLC.

Graduated Master of Marketing from ESCP-EAP European Instituein association with Paris Sorbonne University, along with nearly 10 years of experience in the field of market research, Van is one of INTAGE's dynamic and experienced research director with the ability to advice and handle both Quantitive and Qualitative research projects across the methodologies and industries.

Ms. CÉLINE NHÃ NGUYỄN

Lawyer - Climber
The first Vietnamese woman to conquer Mount Everest

Céline Nhã Nguyễn is a lawyer with 15 years of experience consulting on multiple legal practice areas and advising strategic investment for both Vietnamese and international corporations. Besides her field of expertise, Céline has reached an impressive achievement: Conquered 6 out of 7 (6/7) highest summits of each continent on Earth. In early May this year, Céline successfully unfurled the Vietnamese flag on the summit of Mount Everest - the highest mount in Asia and also "the roof of the world". Céline's story on Everest has left an impression and influence on lots of people: to dream big and to have the courage to conquer their own "Everest", no matter if it is an Everest in work or in life

Ms. LƯƠNG THỊ MỸ HẠNH

Head of Asset Management
