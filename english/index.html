13:30 – 13:35

Opening Performance

13:35 – 14:20
Anphabe deep-dive report: THE BIG SHIFT

This report covers significant movement of Vietnam’s changing workforce, and what it means for our future of work. While each one may be already disruptive, all of them occurring at once causes unavoidable disruption. Yet by understanding the emerging future, we can learn to go through chaos and turn it into transformation for our company performance as well as our employees’ lives.

Speakers:

  • Ms. Thanh Nguyen - CEO & Chief Happiness Officer, Anphabe.
  • Ms. Van Luu – Associate Director, Intage Vietnam LLC.
14:20 – 14:50
C-suite: LEADERSHIFT - EMBRACING THE NEW GAME

The market is changing stormily with new rules emerging. Internally, leaders also can’t lead employees by the old ways. Which strategy for CEOs to lead the new game, both in business and in people management? 4 CEOs of leading businesses from 4 different hot industries join the C-suite panel to share diverse perspectives and practical approaches to:
- Maximize collective intelligence in the new context
- Drive cultural changes at scale
- Empower for excellence under uncertainties

Speakers:

  • Mr. Vaibhav Bhanchawat – Executive Vice President and Business Head, Marico South East Asia.
  • Mr. Angus Liew - General Director, Gamuda Land (HCM) JSC.
  • Ms. Rachel O'Neale - Country Pharmaceutical Organization Head, Novartis Vietnam Co., Ltd.

Moderator:

  • Mr. Le Tri Thong - Vice Chairman & CEO, PNJ Group.

Anpha Talk: FUTURE OF WORK AND LIFELONG LEARNING

Experience sharing and 3 recipes for success from Unilever Vietnam in removing key barriers and transform their learning culture towards the future of work.

Speaker:

  • Ms. Trinh Mai Phuong - Vice President - Human Resources, Unilever Vietnam.

VIETNAM BEST PLACES TO WORK 2021 AWARD CEREMONY
16:00 – 16:15
Dialogue: THE NEST OF CHOICE

Today, employees are not only seeking a workplace to pay the salary or to provide career development. The pandemic has pushed the bounds of their expectation on what employer can do, from arranging vaccination, providing mental care to giving extra support when needed. Employers who play well rising expanded role would have advantages in winning the battle for talents. From Employer of Choice to ‘The Nest of Choice’, how has Nestlé done that? Join Anphabe exclusive interview with Mr. Binu Jacob - General Director of Nestlé Vietnam to learn more.

Speaker:

  • Mr. Binu Jacob - General Director, Nestlé Vietnam.

Moderator:

  • Ms. Thanh Nguyen - CEO & Chief Happiness Officer, Anphabe.

HR Panel: BIG SHIFT – BIG LEAP
