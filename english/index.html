Anphabe’s Vietnam Best Places To Work Virtual Summit 2021 – An annual thought leadership event, bringing updates on the latest talent trends; a forum to share experiences and best practices for CEOs and HRDs. 

22.12 – PROGRAM AT GLANCE VIETNAM BEST PLACES TO WORK SUMMIT

13:30 – 13:35

Opening Performance

13:35 – 14:15
Anphabe deep-dive report: THE BIG SHIFT

This report covers significant movement of Vietnam’s changing workforce, and what it means for our future of work. While each one may be already disruptive, all of them occurring at once causes unavoidable disruption. Yet by understanding the emerging future, we can learn to go through chaos and turn it into transformation for our company performance as well as our employees’ lives.

Speakers:

  • Ms. Thanh Nguyen - CEO & Chief Happiness Officer, Anphabe.
  • Ms. Van Luu – Head of Consumers Insights, Intage Vietnam LLC.
14:15 – 14:45
C-suite Panel: LEADING THE NEW GAME 

Markets change, new rules emerge. Which strategy for companies to lead the new game? 4 CEOs of leading businesses in Vietnam join the panel discussion and explore diverse perspectives on:
- Maximize collective intelligence in the new context
- Drive cultural changes at scale
- Empower for excellence under uncertainties

Speakers:

  • Mr. Vaibhav Bhanchawat – Executive Vice President and Business Head, Marico South East Asia.
  • Mr. Angus Liew - General Director, Gamuda Land (HCM) JSC.
  • Ms. Rachel O'Neale - Country Pharmaceutical Organization Head, Novartis Vietnam Company Limited.

Moderator:

  • Mr. Le Tri Thong - Vice Chairman / CEO, PNJ Group.

Anpha Talk: LIFE LONG LEARNING, EASIER SAID THAN DONE

Experience sharing and 3 recipes for success from Unilever Vietnam in removing key barriers and transform their learning culture towards the future of work.

Speaker:

  • Ms. Trinh Mai Phuong - Vice President - Human Resources, Unilever Vietnam.

VIETNAM BEST PLACES TO WORK 2021 AWARD CEREMONY
16:00 – 16:15
Dialogue: HOME OF CHOICE

In a "giant house" with a large number of employees, departments and distances, how to remove the "walls" created by the heaviness and complexity, and help employees confidently be their best selves? Sharing from Nestlé Vietnam will reveal practical experiences to turn the company into a "safety nest", helping employees feel secure and energized every time they come to the company, like the feeling of returning home.

Speaker:

  • Mr. Binu Jacob - General Director, Nestlé Vietnam.

Moderator:

  • Ms. Thanh Nguyen - CEO & Chief Happiness Officer, Anphabe.

Motivational talk: TIP FOR LEADERSHIP: ACTIVATE SUCCESS

Exclusive speaker: Mr. Mai Duc Chung, Head coach of Vietnam women's national football team; Vice President of the National Coach Council, Former Head of football department of Vietnam General Administration of Gymnastics and Sports, who has led Vietnam women's national football team to make history when winning SEA Games 6 times and record outstanding achievements in regional and continental playgrounds. What makes him successfully lead the team and reaching new heights? Watch the sharing session with tips and best practices, brought to you by Head Coach Mai Duc Chung.
- Striking excellence with limited resources
- Boosting team morale
- Fixing “shocks” & igniting team for peak performance
