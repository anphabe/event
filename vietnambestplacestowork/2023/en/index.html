Ms. THANH NGUYEN

CEO & Chief Happiness Officer at Anphabe

Ms. Thanh Nguyen is the CEO & Chief Happiness Officer of Anphabe – the pioneer in Vietnam in providing integrated Employer Branding (EB) & Happy Workforce solutions. Pursuing the mission of “Building Vietnam Happy Workforce”, in August 2017, Thanh Nguyen has had the honor to be the first Vietnamese to receive Asia HRD Awards for “Contribution to HR Community”, organized by HRD Asia Congress. Prior to her commitment at Anphabe, Thanh has had unique experiences as Regional Marketing Manager at Unilever Asia, Africa, and Middle East region at Unilever and later, as the Founder & CEO of an HR service subsidiary under Navigos Group.

Ms. RACHELE FOCARDI

Chuyên gia hàng đầu về Đa dạng thế hệ và là tác giả sách bán chạy nhất “Định hình lại các khuôn mẫu thế hệ”

International Educator - Ms. Rachele, a global leading expert on Generational Diversity and bestselling author of “Reframing Generational Stereotypes”. With an exceptional sphere of influence, she was voted one of The 6 Most Influential Women in the New World of Work.

Ms. Rachele is not only a Thought leader but also a keynote speaker on Multigenerational Workforce Dynamics, the Future of Work & Employer Branding who has advised hundreds of Fortune 500 companies, NGOs, and Government Agencies for the last 20 years.

Furthermore, she holds pivotal positions such as Chair of the Multigenerational Workforce Committee for the ASEAN Human Development Organization, Board Member of Future Talent Council, Mentor at Workplace Accelerator, and Founder of XYZ@Work. Ms. Rachele is a Former Chief Strategy Officer for Universum and Anchorwoman for CNBC.

Ms. LUU BAO VAN

Intage Vietnam LLC.

Graduated Master of Marketing from ESCP-EAP European Instituein association with Paris Sorbonne University, along with nearly 10 years of experience in the field of market research, Van is one of INTAGE's dynamic and experienced research director with the ability to advice and handle both Quantitive and Qualitative research projects across the methodologies and industries.

In addition to her extensive experience in operating projects and consulting for many big clients, Van is also a lecture teaching Marketing Research, a trainer of Intage Research Training courses for clients, and also being representative Intage Vietnam participating in relavent seminars in Vietnam and international as a speaker.

Bà TRỊNH MAI PHƯƠNG

Phó Chủ Tịch Phụ Trách Nhân Sự Unilever Việt Nam
Kiêm Nhân Sự Ngành Hàng Chăm sóc Cá nhân, Unilever Đông Nam Á

After pursuing a Master’s Degree in Educational Management in Australia, Ms. Trinh Mai Phuong came back to Vietnam and accelerated her career in Human Resources Management. She has 18 years of deep experience in both strategic, expertise and generalist roles for large FMCG companies, in diverse areas of expertise including: learning, talent and leadership development, HR business partnering, strategic HR planning and management.

Having a strong passion for lifelong learning, she strives for continuously innovating HR practices to enable the workforce to adopt agile learning, get prepared for the future and create more values to self and others. She has lived in Ukraine, Russia, Australia, Indonesia and can speak a number of languages. She is a strong advocate for diversity, inclusiveness and agility in the workplace.

Bà NGUYỄN THỊ THU HÒA

Tổng Giám Đốc
ADP Group

Ms. Hoa's journey with ADP began in 2013 when she joined as Sales & Marketing Director. Her dedication and leadership continued to shine, propelling her to the esteemed position of Hanoi Branch Director and Vice General Director, as well as becoming a shareholder of ADP in 2015. She is the key person responsible for ADP's business growth. From 2019 to the present, Ms. Hoa has been the CEO of ADP, where she has refreshed the company and opened a new chapter in its development.

After 9 years, with her profound dedication, ADP’s revenue has significantly increased from VND90 billion in 2013 to VND600 billion in 2022. Ms. Hoa is also one of the founders of Space.s, a member of ADP Group that responds well to clients' demands in Industrial Zones.

As considering humans is the important key that leads to success, Ms. Hoa Being has made a great investment in developing the ADP human resource. With her inspirational leadership, Ms. Hoa has successfully strengthened the staff's connection with the power of trust that helps the company keep thriving even amid the pandemic.

Giáo sư CLAIRE MACKEN

Tổng Giám Đốc
RMIT Việt Nam

Professor Claire Macken is the Pro Vice-Chancellor and General Director of RMIT Vietnam. She has previously held positions as Executive Dean (Academic and Students) (Interim) at RMIT Vietnam and as the Associate Deputy Vice-Chancellor Learning and Teaching in the College of Business and Law at RMIT University in Australia.

Claire’s career spans both higher education and business, having also held roles as a Director of Education at KPMG and at Apple, leading Higher Education and Industry, as well as senior academic roles in universities. Claire is a nationally-award winning teacher, with an Australian Award for Teaching Excellence and an Australian Citation for her work in relation to Student Engagement. Claire holds a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD), a Master of Business Administration, a Graduate Certificate in Higher Education, a Graduate Certificate in Artificial Intelligence in Business, Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Law degrees with Honours and is the author of ten books and multiple book chapters and journal articles in both the areas of law and tertiary education.

Bà ALEXIS PHẠM

Giám Đốc Nhân Sự
Home Credit Việt Nam

Với kinh nghiệm hơn 20 năm trong ngành quản trị nhân sự, Alexis đã đảm nhận nhiều vị trí chiến lược từ trong nước đến khu vực, với chuyên môn chính về Quản Trị & Phát Triển Nhân Tài, Xây Dựng Cơ Cấu Tổ Chức, và Phát Triển Đối Tác Nhân Sự cho doanh nghiệp.

Hiện bà đang nắm giữ chức vụ Giám đốc Nhân sự tại Home Credit Việt Nam. Trước đó, Alexis đã từng là Giám đốc Nhân sự tại Techcombank trong 5 năm, và Giám đốc Nhân sự tại One Mount Group trong vòng 2 năm. Với 2 vị trí trước đó, bà đã cùng đội ngũ Nhân sự thành công xây dựng Techcombank và One Mount Group trở thành những Nơi làm việc tốt nhất Việt Nam (“Vietnam’s Best Places to Work”) trong lĩnh vực Tài Chính, Ngân Hàng và Công Nghệ, Kỹ Thuật Số.

Trước khi gia nhậpTechcombank vào năm 2015, Alexis từng đảm nhiệm vị trí Giám đốc Phát Triển Nhân Tài và Tổ Chức khu vực Châu Á – Thái Bình Dương thuộc British American Tobacco (BAT). Trước đó, bà còn là Giám đốc Nhân sự khu vực Việt Nam, Campuchia, Philippines và Myanmar tại BAT. Kinh nghiệm làm việc đa dạng từ tập đoàn đa quốc gia đến doanh nghiệp trong nước, trải rộng trên các ngành nghề như FMCG, Tài Chính, Ngân Hàng, Công Nghệ và Kỹ Thuật Số giúp Alexis hiểu rõ và dễ dàng xác định cơ hội cũng như thách thức của các doanh nghiệp. Kinh nghiệm trên còn giúp bà cung cấp các chiến lược, giải pháp thức thời, và phương án triển khai hiệu quả giúp tăng giá trị tổ chức nơi bà làm việc.

Alexis tốt nghiệp Đại học Công nghệ Nanyang, Singapore vào năm 2003 với bằng Cử nhân Quản trị Kinh doanh. Bà luôn nằm trong danh sách sinh viên xuất sắc của trường, cũng như đứng đầu khóa học và tưởng thưởng Cử nhân danh dự xuất sắc (First Class Honours).
