“X Factor is the competitive advantage that allows businesses to consistently outperform their rivals. It is the 'wow' factor that captures attention, sparks loyalty, and generates buzz.

The market landscape for 2024 is gradually warming up with positive signals. However, opportunities always come with challenges, raising some tough questions:​​

  • The market started take off again, but is your business being left behind?​
  • Talent is still available, but are they truly engaged?​​
  • Technology is advancing quickly, but are people keeping up?​​

The idea of “The X Factor” now goes beyond individual skills or business advantages; it focuses on the core values and unique vision that organizations can bring, creating a real difference and strong connections with people.

Instead of getting caught up in the fast pace of change, leaders should concentrate on how to take action to build on current strengths and explore new resources, ensuring they can grow and succeed?​

This is what the VNBPTW 2024 is all about.​

Ms. THANH NGUYEN

CEO & Chief Happiness Officer at Anphabe

Ms. Thanh Nguyen is the CEO & Chief Happiness Officer of Anphabe – the pioneer in Vietnam in providing integrated Employer Branding (EB) & Happy Workforce solutions. Pursuing the mission of “Building Vietnam Happy Workforce”, in August 2017, Thanh Nguyen has had the honor to be the first Vietnamese to receive Asia HRD Awards for “Contribution to HR Community”, organized by HRD Asia Congress. Prior to her commitment at Anphabe, Thanh has had unique experiences as Regional Marketing Manager at Unilever Asia, Africa, and Middle East region at Unilever and later, as the Founder & CEO of an HR service subsidiary under Navigos Group.
