 “THE GAME CHANGER” Vietnam talent 2023 landscape

The Vietnamese economy faces many challenges in 2023, such as a low consumer confidence index and widespread layoffs. These challenges affect
not only the business performance but also the psychology and behavior of employees, even in well-performance companies. Companies need to be
creative and smart to overcome these challenges.

The report “THE GAME CHANGER” is a valuable resource for CEOs & HRD who want to stay ahead of the curve in the talent market. It covers the latest trends and insights from the first half of 2023 and provides practical solutions to leverage your human capital, overcome challenges, and become "The Real Game Changer".

Pursuing the mission of “Building Vietnam Happy Workforce”, in August 2017, Thanh Nguyen has had the honor to be the first Vietnamese to receive Asia HRD Awards for “Contribution to HR Community”, organized by HRD Asia Congress. Prior to her commitment at Anphabe, Thanh has had unique experiences as Regional Marketing Manager at Unilever Asia, Africa, and Middle East region at Unilever and later, as the Founder & CEO of an HR service subsidiary under Navigos Group.
